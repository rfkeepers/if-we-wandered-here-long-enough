# Safety

All play benefits from the inclusion of safety tools and systems. The amount you utilize these systems is always up to your table.  The following are particularly recommended by author.

- [CATS](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1mbGczgeDLq_Z-KU8pHHw0gNYlo1NCcZF/view?usp=drive_link)
- [Lines and Veils](https://rpg.stackexchange.com/questions/30906/what-do-the-terms-lines-and-veils-mean)
- [Open Door Policy](https://leavingmundania.com/2014/02/27/primer-safety-in-roleplaying-games/)
- [Script Change](https://briebeau.com/thoughty/script-change/) (particularly, the tools).
- [Stars and Wishes](https://www.gauntlet-rpg.com/blog/stars-and-wishes)

Other tools and resources can be found at [ttrpgsafetytoolkit.com/](https://ttrpgsafetytoolkit.com/).  You are encouraged to use any and all tools that help promote your table's safety, confidence, and overall well being.
